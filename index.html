CEG Tech Forum

CTF - CEG Tech Forum is the nucleus of all technical pursuits in CEG. Conceived in 2006 with the prime motive of encouraging and guiding students in converting their project ideas into industry oriented products, the organization continually works towards inculcating a passion for technology into all of the bright minds at CEG. The organization is certified to the ISO 9001:2015 quality standard.
